Who We Are

A holding company built for India's next decade.

Grevoo was built by Saman Sunasara with a clear thesis: India's biggest problems — employment, healthcare access, immigration complexity, workplace safety, and digital growth for SMEs — deserve purpose-built technology, not generic solutions.

We operate as a parent company to five independent platforms, each with its own mission, team, and product roadmap. Shared infrastructure. Shared values. One uncompromising standard of trust.

Built in Gujarat

Headquartered in Vadodara. Designed for Bharat, not just metros.

AI-Native

Every platform leverages artificial intelligence to reduce cost and increase access.

Self-Funded

No outside capital. Full ownership. Long-term thinking over quick exits.
